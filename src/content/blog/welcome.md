This is the first post on my newly rebuilt site. It's now powered by
[Astro](https://astro.build), which means I can write posts in plain Markdown
and they show up here automatically.

## Why a blog?

Writing clarifies thinking. I plan to use this space for:

- short notes on papers I read,
- write-ups of experiments and tooling,
- the occasional longer deep dive.

## How posts work

Each post is a Markdown file in `src/content/blog/`. The frontmatter at the top
sets the title, date, description, and tags — and the site handles the rest:
listing, sorting, and rendering. Set `draft: true` to keep a post out of the
production build while you work on it.
